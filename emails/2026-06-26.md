### BranchBench Performance Update

A few weeks ago, we [reviewed the BranchBench paper](https://www.dolthub.com/blog/2026-06-03-branch-bench-database-benchmarking-for-agentic-workflows/) published by researchers at the Columbia University DAP lab. The benchmark identified some queries that needed optimizing in Doltgres. We promptly fixed Doltgres to run the queries faster. [Read more here](https://www.dolthub.com/blog/2026-06-25-branch-bench-performance-update/).
