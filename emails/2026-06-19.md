### Git For Context

Speaking of [Git for Context](https://www.dolthub.com/blog/2026-06-15-git-for-context), can I interest you in [a fork of Open Code](https://github.com/dolthub/opencode) that stores your context in Dolt? You can commit, branch, and diff your coding sessions context. We think this is most useful for harness developers but we also think storing your context in a "context hub" could be really useful as well.

I [hit a bug in Dolt](https://github.com/dolthub/dolt/issues/11210) with large JSON blobs containing escape characters when I was demoing our Open Code fork Monday so the demo in the article is kind of short. That's fixed now and I ran a long session yesterday making commits along the way. The [database is on DoltHub](https://www.dolthub.com/repositories/timsehn/opencode-sample-context) and I'll write about it next week.
