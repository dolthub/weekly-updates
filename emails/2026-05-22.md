### Delete Your Comments

As many of you know, my new pet project is [DoltLite](https://github.com/dolthub/doltlite), the SQLite-compatible version of Dolt. DoltLite is entirely [vibe coded](https://www.dolthub.com/blog/2026-03-26-vibe-code-vs-trad-code/). I'm learning a ton about this new way of engineering and I'm eager to share what I learn.
